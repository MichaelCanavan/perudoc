Professor Canavan is a native of Peru and a Professor of Spanish Studies at Northeastern University. Every summer she leads a new group of students on a 5 week adventure through the cities of Peru. They experience the sights, history and culture of the country.

    Peru Dialogue of Civilization

    The Peru Dialogue of Civilization is an amazing Spanish immersion program where students will speak Spanish, participate in cultural activities and interact with local people. It is an exciting way to explore the Spanish language and to experience another culture's people, ideas, customs and beliefs. Students will sample all that the cities of Lima, Ica and Cuzco have to offer using the Spanish language. Students will complete a Service Learning project (Air Force schools) in which they will help Peruvian high school students to practice speaking English and also explain the typical day in the life of a student at an American University. Students from Northeastern will participate in an immersion language program at the Private University UNIFE. The last week of the immersion program features a visit to the one of the seven wonders of the world: MACHU PICCHU!
